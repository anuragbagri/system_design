# Data Governance & Compliance (Advanced)

## Overview

Data governance covers policies, controls, and practices to ensure data quality, privacy, and compliance with regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

## Key Areas

- Data classification and inventory
- Access controls and least privilege
- Audit logging and provenance
- Data retention and deletion policies

## Compliance Considerations

- Data residency and cross-border rules
- Subject access requests and consent management
- Encryption, pseudonymization, and anonymization techniques

## Operational Practices

- Automated policy enforcement (DLP, IAM)
- Regular audits and compliance reporting
- Maintain data lineage for regulatory requirements

## Best Practices

- Design privacy into systems (privacy by design)
- Keep minimal data required and delete when no longer needed
- Provide clear data handling documentation and opt-out controls
